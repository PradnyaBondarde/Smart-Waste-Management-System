About EcoSmart

EcoSmart is a cutting-edge waste management system designed for modern cities. Our platform integrates IoT technology, real-time data analytics, and smart routing to revolutionize how cities handle waste collection and recycling.

With a network of smart sensors, AI-powered analytics, and a user-friendly interface, we're helping cities reduce operational costs, minimize environmental impact, and create cleaner urban spaces.

Core Features

Smart Bins

IoT-powered bins with fill-level sensors, optimizing collection routes and schedules.

Real-Time Tracking

Monitor waste collection vehicles and bin status in real-time through our dashboard.

AI Route Optimization

Machine learning algorithms that optimize collection routes, saving fuel and time.

Waste Segregation

Smart systems for efficient waste segregation and recycling management.
